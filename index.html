about me

Hello! I’m Amr Al-Amawy, a 20-year-old web design enthusiast currently in my third year at Tanta University’s Faculty of Computers and Information.

Based in Egypt, I’m diving deep into the world of front-end development, where I’ve already completed about 50% of my journey in mastering the skills to create engaging, user-friendly websites.

Driven by a passion for technology and creativity, I’m always looking to learn and experiment with new design trends and tools that can elevate user experiences.

This website is a space for me to showcase my progress, projects, and the knowledge I’m gathering along the way. Let’s connect and explore the digital world together!
